Kaedehara Kazuha's favorite dish - All-Weather Beauty

Description

Made from fish that have been dried on the ship. Kazuha claims that they were completely ordinary fish. But this flavor... this must surely be the definition of free, all-embracing nature, no?

Ingredients:

  • Horse mackerels - 2x
  • Water - 1 l
  • Coarse salt - 70 g
  • White radish
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the mackerels;
  2. Add salt to the water and stir it up;
  3. Put the mackerels into the salted water, then get them out and leave to marinate overnight;
  4. Grate the radish;
  5. Fry the mackerels

And that's it!
